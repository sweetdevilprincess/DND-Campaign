# Fairy

### Fairy Traits
A fairy will begin it's life tiny and over time grow until their environment cannot allow more growth. They become more animalistic through this process.

***Ability Score Increase*** Your Charisma score increases by 2.

***Age*** Fairies age similarly to elementals, through trials they age


***Size***
	- Levels 1-5: Tiny
    - Levels 6-10: Small
    - Levels 11-15: Medium
    - Levels 16: Large/Huge

***Speed*** 
	- Levels 1-5: 15 ft walking 30 ft flying. 
    - Levels 6-10: 20 ft walking 20 ft flying
    - Levels 11-15: 30 ft walking hover 10 ft 
    - Levels 16: 25 ft walking
    - Levels 17: 20 ft walking
    - Level 18: 15 ft walking
    - Level 19-20: 5 ft walking 

***Evolutionary Tradeoffs*** 
- As they grow larger, they gain strength but lose flight
- Could gain charm/enchantment abilities to compensate
- Higher level abilities could include:
    - "Call Lesser Fae" - ability to summon smaller fairies
    - "Fae Trance" - ability to entrance other fae creatures
    - Enhanced strength and constitution, but reduced mobility

2. Cultural/Behavioral Elements:

- Solitary except when hunting
- Complex relationship with their own kind
- Perhaps a hivemind-like connection to Queen Fairies
- Natural predators of other fae creatures

***Languages*** Able to speak Sylvan, Druidic, Common. As they grow larger they lose the ability to speak verbally.
